Trend Micro:2015 Summer UX Research Intern

About

I was a UX research intern on Trend Micro – Human Interface Engineering (HIE) team in 2015, researching consumer’s need and providing behavior insights for UI and Visual designers crafting innovative security product.

I got to research across web, mobile, IoT to expand the concept of the product. I also had a chance to lead the video making of the persona for both Taiwan and Japan markets. With the support and guidance of the HIE team, I learned an incredible amount about how design research fit in the different product cycle and how user-centered design mingles within a large picture of business strategy.

Out of respect for company work and NDA regulations, please contact me to learn more!
